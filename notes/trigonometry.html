Definitions

  • sine: sinОё = opposite / hypotenuse

  • cosine: cosОё = adjacent / hypotenuse

  • tangent: tanОё = opposite / adjacent

  • cosec: cosecОё = 1/sinОё

  • sec: secОё = 1/cosОё

  • cotangent: cotОё = 1/tanОё

  • arcsin = sinвҒ»В№
