General

  • Probabilistic automaton: A generalized NFA
    • Like an NFA, but transitions also have associated probabilities
    • Language recognized: Stochastic languages
    • Can be used as operational semantics of a probabilistic system

Bernoulli distribution

A distribution on {0, 1} which takes value 1 with probability p and 0 with probability 1-p is known as a Bernoulli distribution with success parameter p.
