Arithmetic and geometric progessions

Arithmetic progression (AP)

General term: nth term of an AP

  • a: first term
  • d: common difference
aₙ = a + (n-1)d

Sum of first n terms of an AP:

      n
Sₙ = ─── [2a + (n-1)d]
      2

Often abbreviated as GP.

  • r: common ratio

Arithmetic and geometric means

Arithmetic mean of two numbers a and b:

      a + b    
AM = ───────
        2

Geometric mean of two positive numbers a and b:

GM = √(ab)

A relation: AM ≥ GM
