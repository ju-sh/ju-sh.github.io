History of France

  • Ancien régime (Bourbons)
    • Louis XIV (Sun king) (le Rois Soleil)
      • Versailles palace built
    • Louis XVI (Reign: 1774-1791)
      • Treaty of Paris (1783) and US independence from Britain
      • Estates-General of 1789 (Les États-Généraux de 1789)
      • National Assembly (Assemblée nationale) (formed initially by the Third estate)
      • Deposed by the revolution
  • French revolution (1789-1792)
    • Storming of Bastille (Prise de la Bastille): 14 July 1789
  • First republic (1792-1804)
    • La Marseillaise (song) written (1792)
    • Maximilien Robespierre's 'Reign of terror'.
    • Rise of Napoléon Bonaparte
    • Wars 1st and 2nd coalitions.
  • First Empire (Napoléon I) (1804-1814,1815)
    • Napoléon Ier, le Grand (the great)
      • Empereur des Français (Emperor of the French)
    • Sale of Louisina to USA (Vente de la Louisiane) (1804)
      • Included port city of New Orleans
    • Battle of Trafalgar (1805)
    • Napoleonic wars
      • Wars 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th coalitions.
    • Napoleon's return (100 days) and second exile (1815)
      • War of 7th coalition
      • Battle of Waterloo (1815)
  • Bourbon restoration (1814,1815-1830)
    • Louis XVIII (Louis Dix-huitième, le Désiré) (the desired)
    • Charles X (Charles Dixième)
  • Louis Philippe (July monarchy) (Monarchie de Juillet) (1830-1848)
    • aka Orleans monarchy (House of Orleans)
    • Louis Philippe Ier, le rois Bourgeois (the citizen king)
      • Roi de Français (King of the French)
    • Conquest of Algeria from Ottaman Empire (1830-1847)
      • Abdelkader ibn Muhieddine (عبد القادر ابن محيي الدين) attempts to resist
    • French revoluiton of 1848 (February revolution) (Révolution de Février)
  • Second republic (1848-1852)
    • Napoleon III heading a republic
  • Second empire (Napoleon III) (1852-1870)
    • Franco-Prussia war (1870-1871)
      • Battle of Sedan
  • Third republic (La troisième République) (1870-1940)
    • New colonies: Vietnam, Tunisia, etc
      • French Protectorate of Tunisia (1881) (taken from Ottoman Empire)
      • French Protectorate of Morocco (1912)
    • World War I (1914-1918)
      • Battle of Verdun (1916)
      • Battle of Somme (1916)
    • World War II (1939-1945)
      • Maginot line (Ligne Maginot) constructed
      • Battle of France (1940)
  • Vichy France (1940-1944)
    • Headed by: Philippe Pétain
    • Operation catapult: British attack French Africa
      • Attack on Mers-el-Kébir (1940)
    • Operation torch: Allied landings on French Africa
    • Operation overlord: Allied attack on Nazi Europe
      • Operation neptune: Normandy landings
  • Fourth republic (1946-1958)
    • French forces leave Vietnam (1954 Geneva conference)
    • Algerian war (Guerre d'Algerie) (الثورة الجزائرية)
    • Cambodia, Laos independent (1953)
    • Morocco independent (1955)
    • Tunisia independent (1956)
    • Suez canal crisis (1956)
  • Fifth republic (1958-)
    • Évian accords (1962) (between Algerian FLN and French government)
    • Algeria independent (1962)
