Honesty is the best policy

  • Date created: Jul-2021

What is the thing that we all strive for? Is it money? Is it power?

Can’t claim to know the answer to that one..

Contentment. That’s what we seek.

Contentment in all aspects.

Happiness, relationshipts, wealth.

The richest and the poorest person could be the same in terms of personal wealth if they are both content with what they have got.

Though stereotype is in favour of the rich usually being less content with what they have, possibly because there is more opportunity for them to make more money.

Whereas the poor may find it easier to be content with what they have, possibly because they see no chance to make more money.

It is rightfully said that new found wealth is detrimental to one’s peace of mind.

Peace of mind.

For peace of mind, honesty is the best policy.

One should be able to live with oneselves.
