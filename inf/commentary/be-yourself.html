Be yourself

  • Date created: Jul-2021

Never hide your bad side to make someone stay. Show your bad side and see who can stay.

If you choose to pretend to be somebody you aren’t to be in the good books of someone, remember that You can’t always be that someone else.

It’s better to be hated for who you are than be loved for who you are not.

Be yourself. The people who are worth being in your life are those like for who you are, not who they want you to be.

Don’t chase people. Be yourself, do your own thing and work hard. The right people will come to you and stay. ¹

I found the following passage sometime ago but forget from where I got it:

… nothing that is meant for you will ever get away. Love deeply, and without the need to possess or own; let beautiful connections pass through you without attachment, slam your heart into the people and the places and the things that ignite something deep inside of your soul, and I promise, I promise - the right things will stay. You will never lose what is for you. Please don’t ever forget that.

If you strive for something, strive for it because you want to, not because someone else wanted you to.

You are not likely to do great job if you yourself are not convinced that what you are doing is what you need to do.

Accept advice from experienced people whom you can trust, but still don’t fool yourself into thinking that every advice is something that you should necessarily follow.
