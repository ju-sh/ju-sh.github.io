My grasshopper green

A poem that I wrote..

Once upon a time during corona,
Imprisoned at home in qurarantine with stigma,
Lonely with thoughts grim and gloom,
When in came a grasshopper green.

Through the window open did he come flying,
Towards me did he come prying,
Fearlessly did he hop onto my hand,
Soothing me in my loneliness grand.

Twice welcome is help unexpected,
As is a friend unexpected,
Lonely prisoner craved a friend,
And a friend did appear in his front.

Shaped like a bee with coat sembling a leaf,
Alleviate did he my quarantine grief.
Being my company the whole night lean,
Fly away at dawn did my grasshopper green.
