Riddle

Seek the words raw in a club up high in the sky
T'ween the abode of spiders and rodents.
Ask for a family of rubus sans ends
in a sky where the gemini shine bright.

Words there are for days and months.
Letters safe under the wings of the twins
evading the fire that ate words before them.
Read them if you can, and spread 'em if you will.

A snowball, carelessly thrown, became an avalanche
n'avalanche that now needs a sacrifice to avert.
Find the words, that only the cloudy sky can reveal
May the gophers up above guide you to the bright pair.
