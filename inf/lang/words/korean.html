Korean words

Corrections appreciated!

  • 안녕하세요 ('annyeonghaseyo'): 'Hello' or 'Good morning' (a formal 'hi')
    • 안녕 ('annyeong'): hi (informal)
  • 예 ('ye'): yes
  • 아니요 ('aniyo'): no
  • 환영하다 ('hwan-yeonghada'): welcome
  • 감사 해요 ('gamsa haeyo'): thank you
  • 뭐 ('mwo'): what ¹
  • 구급대 ('gugeubdae'): paramedic (as written on ambulances)
