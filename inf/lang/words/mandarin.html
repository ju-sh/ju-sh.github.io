Mandarin words

Simplified Chineses characters.

  • Chinese input
  • Kangxi radicals
  • Ending with 'ma' (嗎) usually means a question.

General

  • Nǐ hǎo (你好): hello
  • nán (南): South
    • Nanjing means southern capital?
  • běi (北): North
    • Beijing means northern capital?
  • Dōng (東): East
    • Dōng wú (東吳) means Eastern Wu (in sanguo China).
    • Dōng mén (东门): east gate
  • Xī (西): West
    • Xī mén (西门): west gate
  • Duì bù qǐ (对不起): Sorry.
  • Méi guān xì (沒關係): It's all right (like as a response to 'sorry').
  • Lǎo shī (老师): teacher
    • Zhè shì wǒmen de lǎoshī (这是我们的老师): This is our teacher.
  • xué shēng (学生 (S)): student
    • tā shì xuéshēng ma? (他 是 学生 吗 ？): Is he a student?
    • Nǐ shì xuéshēng ma?(你 是 学生 吗？): Are you a student
    • Tā men shì xuéshēng (他们 是 学生): They are students
  • Wǒmen shì péngyǒu (我们 是 朋友): We are friends
  • Xiè xiè (謝謝): Thanks.
    • Duō xiè (多谢): Thanks
  • Shì (是): Yes
    • Shì de (是的): Yes / "That's right"
    • Duì (对): yes / right
  • Méi yǒu (没有): no
  • Bù (不): no
  • Shénme (什麼): What
  • Rén (人): people
    • Zhōng guó rén (中國人): Chinese people
  • Jiāng jūn (将军): (military) general
  • Huáng dì (皇帝): Emperor
  • Huáng hòu (皇后): Empress
  • Huáng tàihòu (皇太后): Empress Dowager
  • Kěxí (可惜): 'pity' or 'what a pity..' or 'unfortunately'
  • Wǒmen (我们): us
  • Zi (子): child
  • Tiān xià (天下): the world
  • Tiān mìng (天命): mandate of heaven
  • Mòlìhuā (茉莉花): jasmine flowers
  • Lái la (来啦): coming (as in reply to someone asking you to go to them)
  • Jiàng jūn (將軍): (military) general
  • nǐmen: you (plural)
    • nǐmen hǎo: (greeting a group of people)
  • Zhēn de ma? (真的吗？): really?
    • Zhè shì zhēn de (这是真的): This is true
  • Zàijiàn (再见): bye
  • Nǐ ne ('你呢'): and you? (as in asking same question to another person)
  • Wǒ ài nǐ (我爱你): I love you
  • Mǎshàng huílái (马上回来): be right back
  • Gòngtóng (共同): common/together
  • Huángdi (皇帝): Emperor
    • Dà Qīng Huángdi (大清皇帝): Emperor of the great Qing
  • Huánghòu (皇后): Empress
    • Dà Qīng Huánghòu (大清皇后): Empress of the great Qing
  • Shā (杀): kill
    • Shāle huánghòu (杀了皇后): Kill the empress
  • Nǐ láile (你来了): You have come
  • Xīn (新): New
    • Xīnrén (新人): new people
  • fēi cháng hǎo (非常好): very good
  • jī běn duì huà
  • wǒ yǒu ..: I have ..
    • Wǒ yǒu mǎ (我有马): I have horses
  • wǒ méi yǒu: i don't have …
  • bǐ: pen
  • shǒu jī: phone
  • běi zǐ: cup
  • kōng tiáo: air conditioner
  • xié zì: shoe
  • diàn shì: television
  • nǐ yǒu shén me? What do you have?
  • duō shǎo: more less
  • qián: money
    • Mìng jiùshì qián (命就是钱): Life is money
  • Huā: flower
  • píng guǒ: apple
  • pí jiǔ: beer
    • jiǔ: alcohol
  • duō shǎo qián?: how much (money)?
    • <object> duō shǎo qián?: how much is the <object>?
    • píng guǒ duō shǎo qián?: how much for the apple?
      • píng guǒ sān yuán qián.: apple costs 3 yuan.
      • píng guǒ sān kuài qián.: apple costs 3 yuan.
    • huā duō shǎo qián?: how much for the flower? / how much do you spend? (huā has multiple meanings)
  • yuán: Chinese yen (formal)
  • kuài: Chinese yen (informal) / pieces
  • bā kuài qián: 8 pieces of money
  • bā yuán: 8 yen
  • Dàn gāo (蛋糕): cake
  • Taì guì le (taiguila!): too expensive
  • shīfù (師傅): master
  • ma (吗): a suffix made when posing a question
  • Wèishéme (为什么): why?
  • Méiyǒu rén (没有人): nobody
  • Fāshè (发射): fire (as in a command to fire a weapon)
  • Rúguǒ (如果): if
    • Wǒ shuō "rúguǒ" (我说"如果"): I said "if"
  • piàn (骗): lie / cheat
  • Kuài de (快的): quickly (as if urging someone)
  • Dà dūdū (大都督): grand commander/governor (in an army)
  • Xiàn (县): county
  • Jiǎ de (假的): a fake
  • Zhèng de (正的): original??
    • Tā zhēn de sǐle ma? (他真的死了吗？): Is he really dead?
    • Zhèng de ma? (正的吗?): Really? / 'Is it so?' / 'Is it true?'
  • xìng míng (姓名): name (of a person)
  • 'xìng' or 'xìng shì' (姓氏): Surname
    • Nǐ zhèng de xìng Ma?: Is your surname really Ma?
  • míng (名): Given name (like forename)
  • zì (字): (in the past) 'courtesy name' or 'style name'
  • Zhū (猪): pig
  • Jiàn (剑): sword
  • Nǐ tīng dàole ma? (你听到了吗？): heard that?
  • Wǒ zhīdào (我知道): I know
  • wǒ shì (我是): I am
    • Wǒ bùshì jiékè, wǒ shì yuēhàn (我不是杰克, 我是约翰): I am not Jake, I am John.
  • Jiǔ (酒): liquor
  • Zhùzhái (住宅): residence
  • Qīzi (妻子): wife
  • Suǒyǐ (所以): so / therefore
  • Bùguò (不过): but
  • kě shì (可是): however / but ʳ
  • zhèng shì (正是): exactly! / precisely!
  • Bù kěnéng (不可能): impossible
    • Wàn wàn bù kěnéng (万万不可能): absolutely impossible
  • Kàn dàole ma? (看到了吗？): see that?
  • 他 (Tā): he/him
  • 她 (Tā): she/her
  • Dí (敌): enemy
  • Chá (茶): tea
  • Zhè (这): this
  • Nǐ de xiōngdì (你的兄弟): your brother
  • Shuō (说): say/said
    • Shuō dé hǎo (说得好): well said
    • Shuō Cáo Cāo, Cáo Cāo dào (说曹操，曹操到): speak of Cao Cao and Cao Cao arrives. ⁷
  • Míngbáile me? (明白了么？): understand? (as in 'do you understand now?')
    • Wǒ míngbái (我明白): I understand
  • Bào (报): report (as in something reported by someone)
  • Mǎ (马): horse
    • Rén zhōng Lǔ Bù, mǎ zhōng Chì tù (人中呂布，马中赤兔): Lǔ Bù among men, Red hare among horses
    • 「人中呂布，馬中赤兔！」(traditional) ³
  • Páo (袍): robe
  • Zhǐyǒu wǒ (只有我): only I
  • Tāmen (他们): them
  • Chànggē (唱歌): sing
    • Nǐ chànggē (你唱歌): you sing
  • Dāngdài shǐ (当代史): contemporary history
  • Qíbīng (骑兵): horsemen
  • Suǒyǐ (所以): so
  • Shǐzhě (使者): envoy
  • Duōdàle? (多大了?)? how old (are you)?
  • Ěrduǒ (耳朵): ears
  • Wěibā (尾巴): tail
  • Zhè zhǐ (这只): this one
    • Zhè zhǐ lǎo hǔ yǒu wěi bā (这只老虎有尾巴): this tiger has a tail
  • Lǎohǔ (老虎): tiger
  • Kàn kàn (看看): have a look
    • Wǒmen kàn kàn zhè zhǐ lǎohǔ (我们看看这只老虎): have a look at this tiger
  • Jiàn (箭): arrow
    • Gōngjiàn (弓箭): bow and arrow
    • Jiàn hé gōng (箭和弓): arrow and bow
  • xiǎng (想): think
    • Nǐ xiǎng kàn (你想看): you think and see
  • 10 Nián hòu (10年后): 10 years later
  • Nuò (诺): promise (as in responding to an order from a king)
  • Dagē (ദാഗ്ഹ്) (大哥): elder brother
  • Shídài biànle, dàrén. (时代变了，大人。): Times have changed, my lord. (possibly has a mocking tone)
  • Dān shù (单數): odd number
  • Yīgè (一个): a
    • Yīgè nánrén (一个男人): a man
    • Yīgè nǔrén (一个女人): a woman

  • wǒ zhǎng dà la (我长大了): I grew up
  • Huānyíng (欢迎): Welcome (as in a hotel receptionist welcoming a customer)

  • Wéi (喂): hello (as in when answering the phone)
