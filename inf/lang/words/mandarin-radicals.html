Radical combining meaning

DONE 好: Hǎo (Good)

Consists of:

  • 女: woman => mother
  • 子: child

ie, mother and child being together.

It is good for the mother and child to be together.

是: Shì (Yes)

  • 日 (rì): sun
  • 一 (yī): one
  • 走 (zǒu): run
