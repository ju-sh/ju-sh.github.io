Swedish words

Corrections appreciated!

Swedish and Norwegian languages have a lot of words in common. These two languages, along with Danish, are all derived from Old Norse. ʷ

å is pronounced like 'oww' (ɔː).

  • Man*/*kvinna: man/woman
    • Män*/*kvinnor: men/women
  • och: and
    • män och kvinnor: men and women
    • Liten Agda och Olof Tyste: Little Agda and Olof the Silent ²
    • Förr och nu i Wadstena: Past and present in Vadstena
  • Sverige: Sweden
  • Ja: Yes
  • Nej: No
  • inte: not
    • vet inte: don't know
  • Svenska: Swedish (language)
  • vi ses: see you (as in at then end of a message)
  • mor: mother
  • akta!: 'look out' / 'watch out' / 'careful!'
  • kom igen då: come on
    • kom igen då: come on!
  • va?: what?
    • vad: what
  • jag: I
  • jaså: 'is it'? / 'oh, really?'
  • dig: you
  • lugna dig: calm yourselves down
  • stanna: stay / stop
  • här: here
  • snälla: please
  • tack: thanks
  • bra: good / nice / bravo
  • rymden: outer space
  • farbror / tant: uncle / aunt
  • ensam: alone
  • är: is
  • samma klass: same class
  • svar: answer (as in the answer)
    • svara: answer / response (as in answering)
  • hippa: party (as in partying)
  • mitt rum: my room
  • dem: them
  • först: first
  • andra: second
  • mot: against
  • din: your
    • din hund: your dog
  • är: is / are
    • är ni: are you
  • där: there / where
  • ikke da: not then
