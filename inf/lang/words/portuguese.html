Portuguese words

Corrections appreciated!

  • papo: chat

  • bom: good

    • Bom dia: good day

  • Lisboa: Lisbon (the city)

  • Qual o seu nome?: What is your name?

  • Meu nome é Jack: My name is Jack.

  • Arroba: at the rate of symbol in keyboard (ie, @)

Weekdays

  • segunda / segunda-feira: Monday
  • terça: Tuesday
  • quarta: Wednesday
  • quinta: Thursday
  • sexta: Friday
  • sábado: Saturday
  • domingo: Sunday
