Info

  • 'Dadra and Nagar Haveli' and 'Daman and Diu' got merged into a single union territory.
  • National Capital Region of Delhi is a union territory.
  • Chandigarh, which is the capital of both Punjab and Haryana, is a union territory.
  • Goa used to be a union territory, but is now a state.
  • Jammu Kashmir, which used to be a state, was split into the union territories of 'Jammu and Kashmir' and Ladakh.
  • Puducherry (earlier Pondicherry) consists of:
    • Puducherry
    • Yanam (earlier Yanaon)
    • Karaikal (earlier Karikal)
    • Mahé

National symbols

  • Animal: Bengal tiger (Panthera tigris)
  • Aquatic animal: Gangatic dolphin
  • Bird: Peacock
  • Reptile: King cobra
  • Fruit: Mango ⁴
  • Flower: Lotus
  • Tree: Banyan tree (Indian fig) (പേരാല്‍??)
  • Emblem: A version of the Sarnath lion capital of Ashoka.
    • Motto: सत्यमेव जयते (Truth Alone Triumphs)
  • Calendar: Saka era (first month 'Chaitra')
  • Song: Vande mataram (Sanskrit) by Bankim Chandra Chatterjee ³
  • Anthem: Jana gana mana by Rabindranath Tagore
    • First stanza of the five stanza poem.
    • Original is in Bengali.
    • National anthem is the Hindi version.
  • Pledge
    • Originally written in Telugu by Pydimarri Venkata Subba Rao

Elements:

  • Saffron: Strength and courage of people ³
  • White: Peace and truth (including the Ashoka chakra)
  • Green: Fertility, growth and auspiciousness of land
  • Ashoka chakra (Dharma chakra): has 24 spokes
    • Adapted from the Sarnath lion capital of Ashoka.
