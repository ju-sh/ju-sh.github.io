Blood groups

  • Rhesus factor (Rh factor)
    • A protein, whose presence indicates the '+ve and -ve' in blood groups.
    • Rh- can receive only Rh-
    • Rh+ can receive Rh+ or Rh-
  • Plasma
  • Universal acceptor: AB+
  • Universal donor: O-
  • 8 most common blood groups
    • O+, O-, A+, A-, B+, B-, AB+, AB-
    • So there are others as well?
