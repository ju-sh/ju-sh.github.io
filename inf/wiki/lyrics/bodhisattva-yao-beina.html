Bodhisattva: Yao Beina

One of the intro songs of ‘Empresses in the palace’.

Xiǎo shān chóng dié jīn míng miè,
小   山   重    叠  金  明   灭,
Sunlight shimmers on a picture,

Bìn yún yù dù xiāng sāi xuě.
鬓  云  欲 度 香    腮  雪。
Carved in perspective on bed-screen.

Lǎn qǐ huà é méi,
懒  起 画  蛾 眉,
The hair on her temples appear,

Nòng zhuāng shū xǐ chí
弄   妆     梳  洗 迟。
As clouds which, against her cheeks, lean.

Zhào huā qián hòu jìng,
照   花  前   后  镜  ,
Slowly she rises from her bed;
Paints her brows like a crescent moon.

Huā miàn jiāo xiāng yìng
花  面   交   相    映。
She takes her own time, unhurried;
Gets ready none too soon.

Xīn tiē xiù luó rú,
新  帖  绣  罗  襦,
Her flowered head between two mirrors,

Shuāng shuāng jīn zhè gū
双     双     金  鹧  鸪。
Interlay with face in beauty made.
