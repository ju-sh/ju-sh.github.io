‘Maria’ 愛すべき人がいて
Maria ai subeki hito ga ite
Maria, there is someone I should love

キズを負った全ての者達…
Kizu o otta subete no monotachi…
every person who has been hurt…

~

周りを見渡せば
誰もが慌ただしく
Mawari o miwataseba
Dare mo ga awatadashiku
As I look around

どこか足早に通り過ぎ
Doko ka ashibaya ni toorisugi
everyone hurries past.

~

今年も気が付けば
Kotoshi mo ki ga tsukebe
This year, too

こんなにすぐそばまで
Konna ni sugu soba made
when I noticed,

冬の気配が訪れてた
Fuyu no kehai ga otozurete’ta
the sense of winter came this close.

~

今日もきっとこの街のどこかで
Kyou mo kitto kono machi no doko ka de
Two people and their eyes

出会って 目が合ったふたり
Deatte me ga atta futari
meet somewhere in this city today;

激しく幕が開けてく
Hageshiku maku ga akete’ku
things start to happen passionately.

~

それでも全てには
Soredemo subete ni wa
Still

必ずいつの日にか
Kanarazu itsu no hi ni ka
everything has an ending

終わりがやって来るものだから
Owari ga yatte kuru mono da kara
someday.

~

今日もまたこの街のどこかで
Kyou mo mata kon machi no doko ka de
Two people choose to part

別れの道 選ぶふたり
Wakare no michi erabu futari
in this city today;

静かに幕を下ろした
Shizuka ni maku o oroshita
things drew to a close silently.

~

‘Maria’ 愛すべき人がいて

but finally I’m satisfied by someone

結局何もかも満たされる

‘Maria’ 愛すべき人がいて

‘Maria’ 誰も皆泣いている

~

endings always have a reason
