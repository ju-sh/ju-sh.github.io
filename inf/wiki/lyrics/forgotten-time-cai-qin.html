Forgotten times: Cài Qín

Bèi yí wàng de shí guāng (被遺忘的時光) by Cài Qín (蔡琴)

Source: 1 2 3 4 5 6

是谁在敲打我窗
shì shuí zài qiāo dǎ wǒ chuāng　
Who is knocking on my window

是谁在撩动琴弦
shì shuí zài liāo dòng qín xián
Who is shaking the strings

那一段被遗忘的时光
nà yī duàn bèi yí wàng de shí guāng　
That forgotten time

渐渐地回升出我心坎
jiàn jiàn di huí shēng chū wǒ xīn kǎn
is slowly returning to my heart.

~

记忆中那欢乐的情景
jì yì zhōng nà huān lè de qíng jǐng　
Those happy memories

慢慢的浮现在我的脑海
màn màn de fú xiàn zài wǒ de nǎo hǎi
are slowly coming back to me again.

~

那缓缓飘落的小雨
nà huǎn huǎn piāo luò de xiǎo yǔ　
The gently falling rain

不停的打在我窗
bù tíng de dǎ zài wǒ chuāng
cannot stop hitting my window

只有那沈默不语的我
zhǐ yǒu nà shén mò bù yǔ de wǒ　
as I silently

不时的回想过去
bù shí de huí xiǎng guò qù
look back from now and then.
