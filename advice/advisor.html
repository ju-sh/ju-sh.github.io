You depend on your advisor for:

  • guide towards a proper research direction
  • deciding on realistic long-term and short-term goals
  • funding (often)
    • TA-ship, scholarship opportunities
  • meeting departmental requirements
    • Eg: prepare for quals

Faculty is ideally also a mentor:

  • Help get involved in the community
    • Maybe introduce to others as well
  • Prepare for life after grad school
  • Help land internships

But also seek out more mentors (helps broaden your view): Other students, faculty, etc (SIGPLAN-M is a good way to find a mentor)
