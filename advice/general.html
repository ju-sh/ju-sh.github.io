Tips

  • Integrity

  • It's better to be a bit straightforward instead of beating around the bush.

  • If you didn't understand something that's being discussed, don't allow yourself to pretend to have understood it to wiggle out of the situation.

  • Always make a record of the things that you have read and done.

    • Don't just read stuff, implement what you've read.
    • Helps you know if you really understood it.
    • 'Thinking about new ideas is usually more fun than writing up old ones'1. Don't fall into this trap.

  1. Nudge by Thaler and Sunstein↩︎
