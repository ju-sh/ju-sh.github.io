Good PhD practices

  • Make expectations and duties clear with advisor
  • Keep a research diary to track breakthroughs
  • Always have a structure for advisor meets
    • Have an agenda, shared with prof early on
    • After meeting, write minutes and send to prof for validation

Lessons from MS failures

  • Learn to critically analyze papers
    • What are the novelties?
    • What are its limitations?
    • What problems does it not cover?
    • How can this work be expanded to make it better?
  • Make sure that advisor is someone to whom you can talk freely
