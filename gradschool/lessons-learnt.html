Lessons learnt

  • Be honest with your advisor

  • Never miss a meeting with advisor

    • Even if you are going to get scolded (not an ideal scenario that, but still)

  • It's not the lectures that matter, but the training to become an actual independent researcher.

    • 'Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day, teach him to fish and you feed him for a lifetime.'
    • No one worthwhile is going to care about your grades, really. Only whether you learned.

  • Focus! Don't try to learn everything at once, you won't reach anywhere.

  • Blog. Make notes.

    • Don't seek perfection here. You'll only waste time and it won't lead to a blog post.

  • Go to lab every day.

    • Don't bother working from home/room. It's usually not that productive.

  • Avoid 'Instant Messaging' applications like Whatsapp.

    • They are 'distraction generators'.

  • Use citation management tools: BibTeX
