Choosing advisor

  • old students
  • current students
  • leniency/strictness
    • Strict deadlines
  • coolness
  • health
  • work load
    • Make us work on weekends?
  • punishment meted out
  • extra curricular activities or hobbies
  • terminal and open source affinity
  • work credit claiming tendency
  • contact with academic community
    • Not too self contained or closeted, right?
  • Not too political, right?
  • Doesn't get too personal, right?
  • Character
    • Eg: Leeches on students' work?
  • Reviews
    • Former students
    • Current students
