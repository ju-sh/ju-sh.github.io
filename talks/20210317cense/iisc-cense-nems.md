## Virus
 - **capsid**: a protein shell inside which DNA/RNA resides. Looks like the 'head' of a virus.
 - Tail of virus

Can sense if the capsid has DNA RNA by measuring its weight. Low weight => No DNA/RNA.

Resolution of sensing: 
QCM (Quartz Crystal Microbalance) < SAW (Surface Acoustic Waves?) < MEMS < NEMS


## Bio-Mimetics
 - aka Biomimicry
 - Mimicing the stuff in nature for solving complex problems for humans.

## Piezoelectric materials
 - Solid materials that develops electric charge in response to applied mechanical stress.
 - Vice versa as well. ie, mechanical movement in response to applied electric charge.
