## NEMS
 - Nano Eletro Mechanical Systems
 - Electrical and mechanical functionality in the nano-scale level
 - Like smaller MEMS

## MEMS
 - Micro Eltro Mechanical Systems
 - Microscopes at the micro level
 - Made up of components in the 1-100μm range.

## HEMT
 - High-electron-mobility Transistor
 - A **FET** (Field Effect Transistor)
 - Able to operate at higher frequencies than ordinary transsistors.
 - Has a **heterojunction**: a junction with two materials with different **band gaps** as channel instead of a doped region as in **MOSFET** (Metal Oxdide Semiconductor FET).


### Chalcogens
 - Pronounced /ˈkælkədʒɪnz/
 - Group 16 elements of periodic table
 - Like the term halogens for group 17.
 - Consists of: O, S, Se, Tellurium, Polonium

## Epitaxy
 - A way to produce crystalline layers
 - Crystal growth or material deposition
 - The crystalline film so formed: **Epitaxial film/layer**
 - Semiconductor films for chips are grown epitaxially on wafers.

## MOCVD
 - Metalorganic chemical vapour deposition
 - An epitaxy
 - A chemical vapour decomposition method
 - Used to create LEDs.
 - GaN films are grown on this at CeNSE?

## Semiconducting TMD
 - Transition Metal Dichalcogenide (TMD) monolayers
 - Atomically thin semiconductor layers.
 - Part of the **2D material** family (named so to emphasize their thinness).
