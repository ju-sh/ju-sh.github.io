Travel

  • Thomas Cook multi-money card
  • Forex
    • Documents needed: Passport, PAN card, Flight ticket
    • Only some currencies are globally convertable. USD almost always.
    • Some currencies (eg: INR) place a limit on amount that can be taken abroad
  • Getting hold of foreign money
    • Forex service will ask for ID. Like passport. PAN card is needed.
  • Take appropriate money (+ some buffer). Have an idea of the local expenses for things like:
    • Food
    • Public transportation and taxi
  • Take enough money for general shopping
  • Medicine + prescription
  • Know photography rules. Is taking photos of buildings/people in public places illegal?
  • Availability of public toilets. Are they paid?
  • Availability of potable water
