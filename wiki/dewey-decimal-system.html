Ten main classes:

  • 000: Computer science, information & general works
    • In addition to computer science, used for encyclopedia, newspaper, etc
  • 100: Philosophy and psychology
  • 200: Religion
  • 300: Social sciences
  • 400: Language
  • 500: Science
    • Natural sciences and mathematics
  • 600: Technology
  • 700: Arts and recreation
    • Music, sports
  • 800: Literature
    • Poetry, drama
  • 900: History and geography
  • 920: biographies

  • ✓ Will the Dewey decimal number for a specific book be the same in all libraries?
    • No. It depends on the library. The class will probably be the same, but the exact number may very well be different.
