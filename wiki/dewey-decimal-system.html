Dewey Decimal Classification System

https://www.oclc.org/content/dam/oclc/dewey/versions/print/intro.pdf https://www.library.illinois.edu/infosci/research/guides/dewey/

Ten main classes:

  • 000: Computer science, information & general works
    • In addition to computer science, used for encyclopedia, newspaper, etc
  • 100: Philosophy and psychology
  • 200: Religion
  • 300: Social sciences
  • 400: Language
  • 500: Science
    • Natural sciences and mathematics
  • 600: Technology
  • 700: Arts and recreation
    • Music, sports
  • 800: Literature
    • Poetry, drama
  • 900: History and geography
